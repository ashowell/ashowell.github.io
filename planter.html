  1. Home

Project Objective

Design an indoor planter that optimizes space and sunlight and can be easily expanded if needed.

Overview

The french cleat hanging system allows for the planters to be easily moved to any point on the wall depending on the sunlight needs for each plant. More planters of any size can be made and will be compatible as long as the angles on the planters and wall match. If even more space is needed, the wall itself can be expanded following the same pattern.
